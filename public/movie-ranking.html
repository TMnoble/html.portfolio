The Best Movies According To Noble

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Blacklist

Undoubtedly one of the best series of the last decade. The whole series is escapism at its finest and worth a look.

The Originals

A great show about family while being a testament to the endurance of familial love.

Pirates of the caribbean

It's such a sweeping action-adventure story set in an era when villainous pirates scavenged the Caribbean seas.
